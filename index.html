The Winerz ecosystem is powered by a token economy used to enable betting, payouts, staking and NFT trades. Using a token economy enables seamless smart-contract based escrow of funds, supporting large scale betting and tournaments.

NFT

  • - A yield booster when staking $WNZ tokens
  • - A payout booster when winning a match on the platform
